## 1. Executive Summary & Vision

### 1.1 Project Identity
PRIM'O is not merely a bonus management application; it is a cultural transformation tool for SMEs. The project aims to break the cycle of deferred recognition to establish a culture of instant performance.

### 1.2 The Problem Statement: The Recognition Gap
The current management landscape within SMEs suffers from a critical disconnect between the performance provided and the reward perceived.
* **The Psychological Delay:** A bonus arriving at the end of the month (or year) loses its character as a "thank you" to become a simple element of acquired salary. The emotional link with specific effort is broken.
* **Motivation Decay:** When rewards are too distant in time, the incentive for an employee to give 100% of themselves daily erodes.
* **The Static Competition:** Existing solutions like Sodexo or WiiSmile remain uniform and collective. They do not allow for the valuation of individual excellence at the precise moment it occurs.

### 1.3 The PRIM'O Value Proposition
PRIM'O bridges this gap through an approach based on behavioral psychology.
* **Instant Motivation:** Employers reward specific actions with tokens immediately, providing the "real-time" feedback that the new generation of employees seeks.
* **Seamless Experience:** The application focuses on extreme fluidity so that attributing a bonus is as simple and fast as a thank-you message.

## 4. Ideation & Selection Process

### 4.1 Rationale for Project Selection
The choice of PRIM'O as a tutored project is based on a desire for concrete learning.
* **Professional Experience:** Working on a real client need offers unparalleled professional immersion.
* **Technical Challenge:** Implementing a real-time architecture with Node.js, React, TypeScript, and MongoDB represents a motivating technical challenge.
* **Concept Appeal:** The idea of democratizing a "meritocratic employee benefit club" for SMEs immediately appealed to us with its disruption potential.

### 4.2 Competitive Analysis: The "PRIM'O Edge"
Faced with traditional bonuses that are too far removed from effort, PRIM'O positions itself on reactivity.
* **Traditional Bonuses:** Too slow, too bureaucratic, and often disconnected from immediate individual performance.
* **The "1 Month" Problem:** Waiting 30 days for a reward kills instant motivation.
* **PRIM'O Solution:** Allows for rewards at "any time," maintaining an optimal and continuous level of motivation.

## 5. MVP Technical Scope & Priorities (MoSCoW)

### 5.1 Must Have (The Core Engine)
* **Unified Account Management:** Secure creation and management of organizations and users (Employer/Employee).
* **Token Logic:** Budget-to-token conversion and an instant manual attribution mechanism.
* **Reward Marketplace:** Catalog of partner offers and a promotional code delivery system.

### 5.2 Should Have & Could Have
* **Instant Notifications:** Real-time push notifications to alert the employee of their reward.
* **Audit & History:** Detailed transaction history to allow for precise HR management.
