## 1. Executive Summary & Vision

### 1.1 Project Identity
PRIM'O is not merely a bonus management application; it is a cultural transformation tool for SMEs[cite: 1]. The project aims to break the cycle of deferred recognition to establish a culture of instant performance[cite: 1].

### 1.2 The Problem Statement: The Recognition Gap
The current management landscape within SMEs suffers from a critical disconnect between the performance provided and the reward perceived[cite: 1].
* **The Psychological Delay:** A bonus arriving at the end of the month (or year) loses its character as a "thank you" to become a simple element of acquired salary[cite: 1]. The emotional link with specific effort is broken[cite: 1].
* **Motivation Decay:** When rewards are too distant in time, the incentive for an employee to give 100% of themselves daily erodes[cite: 1].
* **The Static Competition:** Existing solutions like Sodexo or WiiSmile remain uniform and collective[cite: 1]. They do not allow for the valuation of individual excellence at the precise moment it occurs[cite: 1].

### 1.3 The PRIM'O Value Proposition
PRIM'O bridges this gap through an approach based on behavioral psychology[cite: 1].
* **Instant Motivation:** Employers reward specific actions with tokens immediately, providing the "real-time" feedback that the new generation of employees seeks[cite: 1].
* **Seamless Experience:** The application focuses on extreme fluidity so that attributing a bonus is as simple and fast as a thank-you message[cite: 1].
